<p><p>The ceramic base market encompasses various materials, each suited for specific applications. Alumina (Al2O3) is widely used for electrical insulators and substrates due to its excellent thermal and electrical properties. Aluminium Nitride (AlN) excels in thermal conductivity, making it ideal for high-power electronics. Beryllium Oxide (BeO) offers superior thermal performance but is limited by safety concerns. Silicon Nitride (Si3N4) is valued for its mechanical strength and thermal stability, often used in aerospace and automotive applications. Other ceramics include various compounds tailored to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
