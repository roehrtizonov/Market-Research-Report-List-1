<p><p>The Towline Floor Conveyor market is segmented into two main types: on-floor and in-floor towline conveyors. On-floor towline conveyors operate on the facility's surface, offering easy accessibility and flexibility for transporting materials across various areas. In contrast, in-floor towline conveyors are installed below the facility's floor level, providing a streamlined approach that minimizes surface clutter and maximizes space efficiency. Both types are designed to enhance production flow and improve operational efficiency in manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
