<p><strong>The Wave Energy Converters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point Absorbers Devices</li><li>Terminators Devices</li><li>Attenuators Devices</li><li>Oscillating Wave Surge Devices</li><li>Submerged Pressure Differential Devices</li><li>Rotating Mass Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wave Energy Converters (WECs) are classified based on their interaction with ocean waves. Point absorbers are buoy-like structures that capture energy from all directions. Terminators are fixed devices that convert energy from incoming waves. Attenuators are floating structures aligned with wave direction, generating energy along their length. Oscillating wave surge devices exploit surface wave motion. Submerged pressure differential devices utilize pressure changes below the surface, while rotating mass devices harness kinetic energy through spinning components, contributing to the growing renewable energy market.</p></p>
