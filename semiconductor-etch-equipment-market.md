<p><ul><li>Dry Etch Equipment</li><li>Wet Etch Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor etch equipment market is divided into two primary types: dry etch and wet etch equipment. Dry etch equipment utilizes gases to remove material from semiconductor surfaces through processes like plasma etching, offering precise control and high resolution for complex patterns. In contrast, wet etch equipment employs liquid chemicals to etch materials, providing a simpler and often faster process for less intricate designs. Both methods are essential for manufacturing integrated circuits and microelectronic devices, each suited to specific applications.</p></p>
