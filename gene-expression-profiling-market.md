<p><p>Gene expression profiling involves analyzing the patterns of gene activity in cells, crucial for understanding biological processes and diseases. The market comprises several key types: RNA sequencing provides a comprehensive view of transcriptome dynamics by quantifying RNA levels, while real-time quantitative PCR offers precise, time-efficient detection of specific gene expressions. Digital PCR ensures accurate quantification by partitioning samples into numerous reactions, allowing for the detection of rare transcripts. Together, these methods enhance research and clinical applications in genomics and personalized medicine.</p></p>
