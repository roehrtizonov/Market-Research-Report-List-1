<p><p>Food safety testing systems and services encompass various technologies to detect contaminants and ensure food safety. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) identifies genetic material from pathogens with high specificity and speed. Immunoassays utilize antigen-antibody reactions for rapid detection of specific contaminants. Chromatography separates and analyzes complex mixtures, identifying chemical residues. Inductive Coupled Plasma (ICP) measures trace metals and minerals in food samples. Culture media supports the growth of microorganisms for traditional microbiological testing, confirming the presence of harmful bacteria or pathogens.</p></p>
