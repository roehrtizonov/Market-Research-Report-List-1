<p><ul><li>DSRC</li><li>Cellular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) market comprises two primary types: Dedicated Short-Range Communications (DSRC) and cellular networks. DSRC is a short-range technology designed for low-latency communication between vehicles and infrastructure, ensuring reliable real-time data exchange for safety applications. On the other hand, cellular V2X leverages existing mobile networks to support broader coverage and high-capacity communication, enabling various applications beyond safety, such as traffic management and infotainment. Both technologies aim to enhance road safety and improve traffic efficiency.</p></p>
