<p><p>Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) software is categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise EAM software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering complete control over data and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Conversely, cloud-based EAM solutions are hosted on remote servers, enabling easy access from any location and reducing the need for extensive IT infrastructure. This model typically operates on a subscription basis, offering scalability and lower initial costs for businesses.</p></p>
