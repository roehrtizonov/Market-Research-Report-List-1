<p><p>The automatic transmission oil pump market comprises three primary types: internal gear oil pumps, rotary oil pumps, and blade oil pumps. Internal gear oil pumps utilize meshing gears to create fluid flow, offering high efficiency and pressure consistency. Rotary oil pumps operate through rotating mechanisms, providing reliable performance and easy maintenance. Blade oil pumps, featuring movable blades, ensure variable displacement and adaptability to demand. Each type caters to specific automotive needs, enhancing transmission performance and efficiency in various vehicle applications.</p></p>
