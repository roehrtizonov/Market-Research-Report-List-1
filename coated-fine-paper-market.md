<p><p>Coated fine paper is categorized into two main types: gloss-coated and dull-coated paper. Gloss-coated paper features a shiny surface that enhances color vibrancy and sharpness, making it ideal for high-quality prints like magazines and brochures. In contrast, dull-coated paper has a non-reflective finish that reduces glare, providing a more subdued appearance suitable for text-heavy publications and art prints. Both types serve distinct purposes in the printing industry, catering to varying aesthetic and functional needs of consumers and businesses.</p></p>
