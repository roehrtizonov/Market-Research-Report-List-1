<p><ul><li>Handwheel Valve Interlock</li><li>Handle Valve Interlock</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The valve interlock market is primarily categorized into handwheel valve interlocks and handle valve interlocks. Handwheel valve interlocks are designed to prevent the operation of a valve unless specific conditions are met, enhancing safety in industrial processes. They typically feature a mechanical system that locks the handwheel in place. Handle valve interlocks, on the other hand, are used for valves with levers, ensuring that these handles cannot be operated concurrently with certain other equipment, further reducing the risk of accidental operation.</p></p>
