<p><p>Software Defined Storage (SDS) is a modern approach to data storage management that utilizes software-based solutions to control and allocate storage resources, separating storage hardware from its management. This abstraction allows organizations to optimize resources, enhance scalability, and boost flexibility while reducing costs associated with traditional storage systems. SDS is increasingly becoming vital for businesses handling vast volumes of data, as it offers efficiency and agility in managing storage infrastructures.</p><p>The Software Defined Storage Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.3% during the forecast period. Additionally, the rise in big data and analytics applications is pushing organizations to adopt SDS for better data processing capabilities.
