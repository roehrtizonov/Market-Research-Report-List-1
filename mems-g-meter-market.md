<p><p>The MEMS G Meter market is categorized into three types based on the number of axes: 1 Axis, 2 Axis, and 3 Axis. A 1 Axis G Meter measures acceleration along a single direction, suitable for basic applications. The 2 Axis G Meter tracks motion in two directions, offering greater versatility for more complex scenarios. The 3 Axis G Meter measures acceleration across three perpendicular axes, providing comprehensive motion analysis essential for advanced applications in automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics industries.</p></p>
