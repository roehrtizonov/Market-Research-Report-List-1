<p><ul><li>Water Based Low Temperature Coating</li><li>Solvent Based Low Temperature Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low temperature coating market is divided into two primary types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based low temperature coatings use water as a primary solvent, offering environmental benefits and low VOC emissions, making them suitable for various applications where safety and sustainability are prioritized. In contrast, solvent-based low temperature coatings utilize organic solvents, providing excellent adhesion and durability for demanding environments. Both types are designed to cure at lower temperatures, enhancing efficiency in processes where heat-sensitive substrates are present.</p></p>
