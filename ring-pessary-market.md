<p><p>The ring pessary market is categorized into three main types: PVC, PP, and others. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) ring pessaries are widely used for their flexibility and durability, making them ideal for pelvic support. PP (Polypropylene) ring pessaries offer a lighter alternative, providing effective support with less irritation. Other materials may include silicone or latex, catering to specific patient needs and preferences. This diversity allows healthcare providers to tailor treatments based on individual patient comfort and clinical requirements.</p></p>
