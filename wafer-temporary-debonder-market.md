<p><p>The Wafer Temporary Debonder market is categorized into several types based on the debonding methods utilized. Thermal debonding employs heat to separate wafers from substrates, enhancing yield while minimizing defects. Mechanical debonding uses physical force for separation, offering precise control. Laser debonding involves focused light to induce stress, allowing for precise and damage-free detachment. Other methods may include chemical or innovative hybrid techniques, catering to specific applications and requirements in the semiconductor industry, enhancing versatility and efficiency in wafer processing.</p></p>
