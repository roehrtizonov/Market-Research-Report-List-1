<p><strong>The Full Colour LED Displays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD</li><li>COB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full Colour LED displays are categorized mainly into SMD (Surface Mount Device) and COB (Chip on Board). SMD displays use individual LED chips mounted on a circuit board, allowing for high resolution and flexibility in design, making them ideal for indoor and outdoor applications. In contrast, COB technology integrates multiple LED chips directly onto a substrate, offering a more compact design with improved thermal management and durability, suited for high-density installations and specialized uses.</p></p>
