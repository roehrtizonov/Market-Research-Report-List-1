<p><ul><li>Baechu-kimchi</li><li>Dongchimi</li><li>Kkakdugi</li><li>Pa-kimchi</li><li>Oi Sobagi</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kimchi market types vary based on ingredients and preparation methods. Baechu-kimchi features Napa cabbage and a spicy seasoning mix, popular for its robust flavor. Dongchimi is a mild, watery radish kimchi, ideal for colder seasons. Kkakdugi consists of cubed radish kimchi, offering a crunchy texture. Pa-kimchi involves green onions, adding a fresh, aromatic twist. Oi Sobagi is cucumber kimchi, known for its refreshing taste and crispy bite, making it a favorite alongside various dishes. Each type caters to different palates and occasions.</p></p>
