<p><strong>The Light Ice Cream Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft Ice Cream</li><li>Hard Ice Cream</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light ice cream market primarily consists of two types: soft ice cream and hard ice cream. Soft ice cream is characterized by its airy texture, produced through a higher incorporation of air and lower fat content, making it smoother and lighter. It is often served immediately after production, typically at ice cream parlors. In contrast, hard ice cream has a denser consistency due to lower air content and higher fat, requiring freezing before serving, resulting in a richer and creamier experience.</p></p>
