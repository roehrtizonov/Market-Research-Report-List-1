<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Pressure Type</li><li>Often Streaming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic booster market can be categorized primarily into two types: Atmospheric Pressure Type and Often Streaming. The Atmospheric Pressure Type operates by utilizing ambient atmospheric pressure to enhance hydraulic pressure efficiently, making it suitable for various applications with low operational costs. In contrast, the Often Streaming type continuously maintains high pressure during operations, ensuring a steady hydraulic flow. This type is crucial for applications requiring consistent performance, offering enhanced efficiency and reliability in demanding industrial environments.</p></p>
