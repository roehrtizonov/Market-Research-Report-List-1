<p><p>Digital textile printing encompasses various techniques, each suited for different fabric types and applications. Sublimation printing utilizes heat to transfer dye onto polymer-based fabrics, ideal for vibrant designs. Pigment printing applies inks directly, offering versatility across different materials. Reactive printing involves a chemical bond with fibers, perfect for cellulosic fabrics and producing vivid colors. Acid printing is tailored for protein fibers like silk and wool, using acid-based dyes for rich hues. Other methods include eco-friendly and specialty inks, expanding market options.</p></p>
