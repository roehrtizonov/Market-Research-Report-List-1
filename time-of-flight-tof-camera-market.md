<p><p>The Time of Flight (ToF) camera market is primarily segmented into CMOS and CCD time-of-flight cameras. CMOS ToF cameras utilize complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, offering advantages like lower power consumption, faster frame rates, and compact designs, making them suitable for portable devices and consumer electronics. In contrast, CCD ToF cameras leverage charge-coupled device technology, known for higher image quality and sensitivity in low-light conditions, making them ideal for industrial applications and scenarios requiring precise depth measurements.</p></p>
