<p><strong>The Digital CZT-Based Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-conversion Type</li><li>Photoconductive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital CZT-Based Detectors market is primarily categorized into two types: direct-conversion and photoconductive types. Direct-conversion detectors utilize Cadmium Zinc Telluride (CZT) crystals to directly convert incoming X-ray or gamma-ray photons into electrical signals, enabling high-resolution imaging with improved sensitivity. In contrast, photoconductive detectors rely on the change in conductivity of materials when exposed to radiation, offering advantages in certain applications. Both types serve critical roles in medical imaging, industrial inspection, and nuclear security by enhancing detection capabilities.</p></p>
