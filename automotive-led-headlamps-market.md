<p><p>The automotive LED headlamp market features three main types: semi-enclosed headlamps, closed headlamps, and projection headlamps. Semi-enclosed headlamps combine elements of both open and closed designs, offering improved aerodynamics and styling. Closed headlamps are fully sealed units that enhance light output and protect against environmental factors. Projection headlamps utilize a lens to focus light beams, providing superior illumination and precise light distribution. These variations cater to different vehicle designs and consumer preferences, enhancing functionality and aesthetics.</p></p>
