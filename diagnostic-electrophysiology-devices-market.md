<p><p>The diagnostic electrophysiology devices market encompasses various technologies used to diagnose and monitor electrical activity in the body. X-Ray systems provide imaging to visualize internal structures, while Electrocardiogram (ECG) devices record heart electrical activity. Diagnostic Electrophysiology (EP) catheters are specialized tools for mapping and treating arrhythmias. Electromyography (EMG) devices assess muscle electrical activity, and Electroencephalogram (EEG) devices monitor brain electrical impulses. Together, these devices are crucial for identifying and managing various medical conditions related to the nervous and cardiovascular systems.</p></p>
