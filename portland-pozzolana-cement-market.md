<p><p>Portland Pozzolana Cement (PPC) is categorized into two main types: artificial and natural pozzolana cement. Artificial pozzolana cement is produced by mixing Portland cement with pozzolanic materials like fly ash or slag, enhancing its strength and durability. In contrast, natural pozzolana cement derives from naturally occurring volcanic ash or similar materials, offering excellent resistance to chemical attacks. Both types are favored in construction for their eco-friendliness, cost-effectiveness, and performance enhancements in concrete applications.</p></p>
