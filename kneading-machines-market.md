<p><p>The kneading machines market is categorized primarily into spiral kneaders, planetary kneaders, and others. Spiral kneaders are designed for high-volume dough preparation, employing a spiraled hook to efficiently mix and knead, ideal for bread production. Planetary kneaders utilize a rotating bowl and different beaters, allowing for versatile mixing tasks, suitable for various types of dough and batters. "Others" encompasses specialized kneading equipment, including double-arm kneaders and continuous kneaders, catering to niche applications in the food processing industry.</p></p>
