<p><p>The de-icers market primarily includes two types: pneumatic and electrothermal de-icers. Pneumatic de-icers utilize compressed air to effectively remove ice and snow from aircraft surfaces, enhancing safety during takeoff and landing. In contrast, electrothermal de-icers use electrical heating elements to melt ice, ensuring efficient operation without the need for mechanical forces. Both types are essential in the aviation industry, providing reliable solutions to maintain aircraft performance and safety in adverse weather conditions.</p></p>
