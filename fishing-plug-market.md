<p><p>The fishing plug market is primarily divided into two categories: deep-diving and shallow-diving plugs. Deep-diving fishing plugs are designed to reach greater depths, targeting species that inhabit lower water layers, making them effective for deep-sea fishing. In contrast, shallow-diving plugs are ideal for surface or near-surface fishing, attracting fish in shallower waters, such as lakes and coastal areas. Both types cater to different fishing techniques and environments, allowing anglers to choose based on their specific needs and the fish species they target.</p></p>
