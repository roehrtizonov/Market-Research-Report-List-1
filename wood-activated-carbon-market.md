<p><p>The wood activated carbon market is primarily segmented into two types: powdered and granular. Powdered activated carbon (PAC) consists of fine particles, making it highly effective for applications requiring fast adsorption, such as in air and water treatment processes. Granular activated carbon (GAC), on the other hand, features larger particles that provide a higher surface area and longer contact time, suitable for continuous flow applications like drinking water purification and industrial processes. Both types serve essential roles in environmental and industrial applications.</p></p>
