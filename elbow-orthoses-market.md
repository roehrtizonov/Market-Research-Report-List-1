<p><p>The elbow orthoses market is primarily divided into two types: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to facilitate movement and support rehabilitation, often incorporating adjustable mechanisms to allow for active muscle engagement and joint mobility. In contrast, static orthotics are meant to immobilize the elbow joint, providing support and preventing movement to aid in recovery from injuries or surgeries. Both types serve distinct purposes in managing conditions affecting the elbow, enhancing patient outcomes through targeted support.</p></p>
