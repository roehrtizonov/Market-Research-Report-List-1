<p><ul><li>Long-term Absorbable Sutures</li><li>Short-term Absorbable Sutures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The absorbable synthetic suture market is categorized into two main types based on their absorption duration: long-term absorbable sutures and short-term absorbable sutures. Long-term absorbable sutures are designed to provide support for extended healing periods, typically dissolving over several weeks to months, making them suitable for complex surgeries. In contrast, short-term absorbable sutures absorb quickly, usually within days, and are ideal for simpler procedures where immediate tissue support is needed, minimizing the need for suture removal.</p></p>
