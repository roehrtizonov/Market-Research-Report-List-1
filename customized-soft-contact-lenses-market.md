<p><p>The customized soft contact lenses market comprises primarily two types: silicone hydrogel and hydrogels. Silicone hydrogel lenses are designed to allow more oxygen to pass through to the cornea, promoting eye health and comfort. They are popular for extended wear due to their durability and moisture retention. On the other hand, traditional hydrogels provide a softer feel and are more flexible, making them easier to adapt to for many wearers. Both types cater to individual vision needs and comfort preferences.</p></p>
