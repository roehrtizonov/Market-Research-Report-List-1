<p><p>The percutaneous nephroscope market consists of two primary types: rigid and flexible nephroscopes. Rigid nephroscopes are solid, providing stability during procedures like kidney stone removal, making them ideal for straightforward access. Flexible nephroscopes, on the other hand, offer greater maneuverability, allowing access to complex renal anatomy and enhancing visualization. Both types cater to distinct medical needs, with rigid instruments being favored for certain conditions, while flexible devices are preferred for their adaptability and ease of use in delicate situations.</p></p>
