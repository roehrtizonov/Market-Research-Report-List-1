<p><p>The off-grid solar energy system market is divided into two main types: Partial Off-Grid and Completely Off-Grid Systems. Partial Off-Grid Systems are designed to supplement existing energy sources, allowing users to partially rely on solar power while still connecting to a traditional grid. In contrast, Completely Off-Grid Systems operate independently, using solar panels, batteries, and inverters to provide all energy needs without any connection to the grid, making them ideal for remote areas or for those seeking energy independence.</p></p>
